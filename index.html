Band Bio

Foo Fighters is a rock band, formed in Seattle, Washingtion in 1994. They were founded by Dave Grohl, former drummer of the Alt Band, Nirvana. Dave Grohl started the band after Nirvana disbanned, due to the death of Nirvana's lead singer, Curt Cobain.

The band got it's name from the UFOs and a variety of aerial phenomenom that were reported by Allied aircraft pilots in World War II. In October 2019, the Foo Fighters announced that they would be recording their tenth studio album.
